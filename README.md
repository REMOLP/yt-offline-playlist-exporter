## Export YouTube playlists effortless and keep the bloat away! (plus some neat, minor features)

---

Very simple playlist exporter. I'm very mad at what YouTube is doing with the site, so and the bloat is just enormous. **I just wanna listen to the songs in my goddamn playlist!**

### IMPORTANT!!!
You already know that often YT includes the unnecessary bs when looking at the playlist. Make sure to delete these "Recommended" etc. stuff in the playlist added by itself that isn't the part of the playlist using dev tools. Then hit the export button.
